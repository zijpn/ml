Hypothesis
$$ h_{\theta}(x) = g(\theta^{T}x)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-\theta^{T}x}} = P(y=1|x;\theta) $$
Regularized Cost Function
$$ J(\theta)=-\frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^{m}\left[y^{(i)}\log(h_{\theta}(x^{(i)}))+(1-y^{(i)})\log(1-h_{\theta}(x^{(i)}))\right] + \frac{\lambda}{2m}\sum_{j=1}^{n}\theta_j^{2} $$
Gradient Descent
$$ \begin{align*} \theta_0 := & \theta_0 - \alpha \frac{1}{m} \sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}(h_\theta(x^{(i)}) - y^{(i)}) x_0^{(i)} \\ \theta_j := & \theta_j(1-\alpha\frac{\lambda}{m}) - \alpha \frac{1}{m} \sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}(h_\theta(x^{(i)}) - y^{(i)}) x_j^{(i)} \\ &(j=1,...n) \end{align*} $$
